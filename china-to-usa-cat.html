How to Bring a Cat from China to the United States

This guide explains the requirements for transporting a cat from China to the United States in 2026.

Basic Requirements

  • ISO compatible microchip
  • Rabies vaccination certificate
  • Health certificate issued by a licensed veterinarian
  • CDC dog import form (if applicable)

Steps to Prepare

  1. Microchip your cat
  2. Get rabies vaccination
  3. Obtain a veterinary health certificate
  4. Prepare airline travel crate
  5. Book pet travel with airline

Airline Travel

Most airlines allow cats either in cabin or as checked baggage depending on weight and airline rules.

Estimated Timeline

Preparing documents usually takes 1–3 months depending on vaccination schedule.
