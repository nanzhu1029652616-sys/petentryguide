Should You Use a Pet Relocation Agency?

Last updated: 2026-03-04

When to hire an agency: 1. You are flying a large dog as manifest cargo. 2. You are navigating high-risk CDC permit countries. 3. You don't have time for endorsements. They handle 'Weather Ban' monitoring and local customs brokerage, which are common points of failure.

Essential Preparation

  • ISO 11784/11785 compliant microchip
  • Rabies vaccination (at least 30 days before travel)
  • International health certificate (endorsed by local authorities)
  • IATA approved travel crate
