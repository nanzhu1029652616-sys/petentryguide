Bring a Cat from Hong Kong to the USA (2026 Guide)

Last updated: 2026-03-04

To bring a cat from Hong Kong, you need an ISO microchip and a valid rabies certificate. Process: You must apply for an Export Health Certificate from the Agriculture, Fisheries and Conservation Department (AFCD). It is recommended to book the AFCD official vet inspection at the airport (HKG) or a local office about 7-10 days before your flight. Cats do not require a CDC permit for the USA.

Essential Preparation

  • ISO 11784/11785 compliant microchip
  • Rabies vaccination (at least 30 days before travel)
  • International health certificate (endorsed by local authorities)
  • IATA approved travel crate
