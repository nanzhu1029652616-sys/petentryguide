Crate Training for Flight: 7-Day Plan

Last updated: 2026-03-04

Crate training is the #1 way to reduce flight anxiety. Accelerated Plan:

  • Day 1-2: Feed all meals inside the crate with the door open.
  • Day 3-4: Close the door for 10-minute intervals while the pet eats.
  • Day 5-6: Leave the pet in the crate in a different room for 1 hour.
  • Day 7: Practice a 15-minute car ride in the crate.
Familiarity prevents panic during the actual flight.

Essential Preparation

  • ISO 11784/11785 compliant microchip
  • Rabies vaccination (at least 30 days before travel)
  • International health certificate (endorsed by local authorities)
  • IATA approved travel crate
