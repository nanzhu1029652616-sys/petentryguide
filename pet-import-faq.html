Q: Does my pet need a rabies titer test? A: Generally no for US mainland, but yes for Hawaii. Q: Can I fly a snub-nosed dog? A: Many airlines ban them; check specialized shippers. Q: Is there a limit on pets? A: No federal limit for personal pets, but 2+ may trigger inspection.
