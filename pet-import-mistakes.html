Top Pet Import Mistakes That Cause Delays

Last updated: 2026-03-04

Even experts make mistakes. Avoid these top 3 errors: 1. Microchip Order: The microchip MUST be implanted or scanned *before* the rabies vaccine is given. 2. Crate Size: If the pet's ears touch the top of the crate, the airline will deny boarding. 3. Endorsement Timing: Government stamps often have a strict 10-day validity window; getting them too early is as bad as getting them too late.

Essential Preparation

  • ISO 11784/11785 compliant microchip
  • Rabies vaccination (at least 30 days before travel)
  • International health certificate (endorsed by local authorities)
  • IATA approved travel crate
