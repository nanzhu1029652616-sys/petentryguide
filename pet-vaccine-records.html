How to Keep Vaccine Records Acceptable for Airlines

Last updated: 2026-03-04

Airlines are often stricter than the government. Record Standard:

  • Must show the pet's name, breed, and microchip number.
  • Must list the vaccine manufacturer, batch/lot number, and expiration date.
  • Must be signed by a licensed vet with their license number and clinic stamp.
Digital records are great, but always carry three physical copies during international travel.

Essential Preparation

  • ISO 11784/11785 compliant microchip
  • Rabies vaccination (at least 30 days before travel)
  • International health certificate (endorsed by local authorities)
  • IATA approved travel crate
