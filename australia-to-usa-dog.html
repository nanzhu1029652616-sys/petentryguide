Bring a Dog from Australia to the USA (2026 Guide)

Australia is a low-risk country for rabies. The 2026 Process: 1. Your dog must be 6 months old and microchipped. 2. Obtain an export health certificate endorsed by DAFF. 3. Submit the online CDC Dog Import Form before arrival. Because Australia is rabies-free, you do not need a CDC Dog Import Permit or a rabies titer test to enter the United States.
