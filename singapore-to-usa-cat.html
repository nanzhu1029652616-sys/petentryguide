Bring a Cat from Singapore to the USA (2026)

Expertly reviewed on 2026-03-04 • 5 min read

Singapore is recognized by the CDC as a rabies-free country. The process is simple: your cat needs an ISO microchip and an export health certificate issued by NParks (Animal & Veterinary Service). While the US doesn't strictly require a rabies vaccine for cats from Singapore, it is highly recommended to avoid any issues with transiting countries or airlines.
