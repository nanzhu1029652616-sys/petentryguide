Airport Security with Pets: TSA Process Explained

Updated: 2026-03-04 • 2026 Guide

TSA Process: 1. Remove the pet from the carrier. 2. Walk through the metal detector with your pet in your arms. 3. Your hands may be swabbed for explosive residue. 4. The empty carrier goes through the X-ray. 5. Place the pet back in the carrier immediately.
