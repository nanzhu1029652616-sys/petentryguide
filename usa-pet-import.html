How to Import a Pet to the United States

This guide explains the requirements for bringing a cat or dog into the United States.

Key Requirements

  • Rabies vaccination
  • Microchip identification
  • Health certificate
  • CDC dog import rules

Required Documents

  • Rabies vaccination certificate
  • Pet health certificate
  • Owner declaration form

Preparation Timeline

Preparing pet travel to the United States usually takes 1–3 months depending on vaccination and documentation.

Airline Pet Travel

Many airlines allow pets in cabin or cargo depending on weight and destination rules.
