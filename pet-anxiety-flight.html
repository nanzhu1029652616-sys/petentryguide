How to Reduce Pet Anxiety When Flying

Anxiety is the biggest challenge. Training is Key: 1. Crate training should begin 30 days early. 2. Place a shirt with your scent inside for comfort. 3. Use calming music during practice. 4. Practice car rides in the crate. A calm owner leads to a calmer pet.
