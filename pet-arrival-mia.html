MIA Pet Arrival Guide (Miami)

At MIA: Head to the North Terminal (D) or South Terminal (J) for customs. MIA is pet-friendly but can be extremely hot; ensure your pet has water. Cargo terminals are located on the west side of the airport perimeter. MIA handles high volume from South America.
