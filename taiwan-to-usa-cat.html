Bring a Cat from Taiwan to the USA (2026)

Expert Relocation Guide • Updated Mar 04, 2026

Taiwan is a low-risk region. Your cat needs a 15-digit ISO microchip and a rabies vaccination certificate. You must obtain an export health certificate from the Animal and Plant Health Inspection Agency (APHIA) within 10 days of travel. No US quarantine is required.
