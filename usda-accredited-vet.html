USDA-Accredited Vet: When It Matters

Verified Guide • Updated Mar 2026

For pets leaving the United States, a USDA-accredited vet is essential. Only these vets can sign official international health certificates that the USDA will subsequently endorse. You can find accredited vets via the APHIS National Veterinary Accreditation Program website.

Quick Checklist

1. ISO Microchip (15-digit)
2. Rabies Vaccination Certificate
3. CDC Dog Import Form (if applicable)
4. Airline Reservation
