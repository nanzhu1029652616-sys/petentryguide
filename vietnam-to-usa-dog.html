Bring a Dog from Vietnam to the USA (2026 Guide)

Last updated: 2026-03-04

High-Risk Alert: Vietnam is a high-risk rabies country. 2026 Requirements: 1. Dog must be at least 6 months old. 2. 15-digit ISO microchip is mandatory. 3. You must have a CDC Dog Import Permit or a reservation at a CDC-approved care facility. A DAH-endorsed health certificate from Vietnam is required for airline check-in.

Essential Preparation

  • ISO 11784/11785 compliant microchip
  • Rabies vaccination (at least 30 days before travel)
  • International health certificate (endorsed by local authorities)
  • IATA approved travel crate
