Do You Need a Pet Passport to Enter the USA?

Expert Relocation Guide • Updated Mar 04, 2026

The 'EU Pet Passport' is an acceptable form of identification for entry from Europe. For pets from Asia or South America, there is no official 'passport'. Instead, you rely on the International Health Certificate and vaccination booklet. The US does not issue its own pet passport.
