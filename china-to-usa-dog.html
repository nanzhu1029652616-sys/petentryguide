Bring a Dog from China to the USA (2026 Guide)

Expert Relocation Guide • Updated Mar 04, 2026

Dogs from China are subject to strict CDC rules as China is classified as a high-risk country for rabies. For 2026 travel: Your dog must be at least 6 months old, have an ISO microchip, and you must apply for a CDC Dog Import Permit at least 6 weeks in advance. Alternatively, you can arrive via a CDC-approved animal care facility if you have a valid CDC Vaccination Reservation.
