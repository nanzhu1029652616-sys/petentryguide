Do Pets Need Deworming for US Entry?

Updated: 2026-03-04 • 2026 Guide

The US federal government does not strictly require internal or external deworming for pet entry. However, your pet must appear free of screwworms and ticks during inspection. It is standard best practice to deworm your pet 5 days before flight to pass the visual health check.
