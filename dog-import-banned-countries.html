Dog Import Restrictions and High-Risk Countries

Last updated: 2026-03-04

The CDC maintains a list of over 100 'High-Risk' countries (including much of Asia and South America). Dogs from these countries face limited ports of entry and stricter paperwork. Always check the current high-risk list 3 months before your planned travel.

Essential Preparation

  • ISO 11784/11785 compliant microchip
  • Rabies vaccination (at least 30 days before travel)
  • International health certificate (endorsed by local authorities)
  • IATA approved travel crate
