Pet Vaccination Checklist for International Travel

Last updated: 2026-03-04

Your vaccination record must be perfect to avoid delays. 2026 Essential Checklist:

  • Rabies: Must be given after the microchip and at least 30 days before travel for primary shots.
  • DHPP/FVRCP: Highly recommended by airlines and US vets for long-term health.
  • Leptospirosis: Often recommended for dogs moving to humid US states.
Always ensure the vet signs and stamps every record in English.

Essential Preparation

  • ISO 11784/11785 compliant microchip
  • Rabies vaccination (at least 30 days before travel)
  • International health certificate (endorsed by local authorities)
  • IATA approved travel crate
