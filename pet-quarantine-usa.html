Does the USA Quarantine Pets? What Actually Happens

For most healthy pets with correct paperwork, there is no mandatory quarantine in the USA. Inspection happens at the first port of entry. Quarantine only occurs if the pet shows signs of illness or if high-risk country documents are missing. Hawaii has its own laws.
