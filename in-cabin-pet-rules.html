In-Cabin Pet Rules: Weight Limits and Booking Tips

Last updated: 2026-03-04

Traveling with your pet in the cabin is the least stressful option. Weight Limits: Most airlines limit in-cabin travel to small dogs and cats weighing under 8kg (18lbs) including the carrier.

  • Soft-sided carriers are preferred as they fit under the seat.
  • You must reserve a 'Pet in Cabin' slot early, as space is very limited.
  • Pets must remain inside the carrier for the entire duration of the flight.

Essential Preparation

  • ISO 11784/11785 compliant microchip
  • Rabies vaccination (at least 30 days before travel)
  • International health certificate (endorsed by local authorities)
  • IATA approved travel crate
