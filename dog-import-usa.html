Detailed Regulations

The CDC heavily regulates dog imports. Core Rules: 1. Every dog must be at least 6 months old. 2. Every dog must have a 15-digit ISO-compliant microchip. 3. Every owner must file a CDC Dog Import Form online. High-risk countries require a Permit.
