Bring a Dog from South Korea to the USA (2026 Guide)

Last updated: 2026-03-04

South Korea is a low-risk country for rabies. 2026 Protocol: 1. Ensure your dog is at least 6 months old and has an ISO-compliant microchip. 2. Complete the mandatory CDC Dog Import Form online at least 2 days before flying. 3. Obtain an export health certificate from the Animal and Plant Quarantine Agency (QIA). No CDC Import Permit or rabies titer test is required for dogs from South Korea.

Essential Preparation

  • ISO 11784/11785 compliant microchip
  • Rabies vaccination (at least 30 days before travel)
  • International health certificate (endorsed by local authorities)
  • IATA approved travel crate
