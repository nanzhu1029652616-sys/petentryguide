Bring a Dog from South Korea to the USA (2026)

Expert Relocation Guide • Updated Mar 04, 2026

South Korea is a low-risk country for rabies. Key steps: 1. Ensure your dog is at least 6 months old and has an ISO microchip. 2. Complete the online CDC Dog Import Form at least 2 days before flying. 3. Obtain a QIA-endorsed export health certificate. No CDC Import Permit or rabies titer test is needed for dogs originating from South Korea.
