SFO Pet Arrival Guide (San Francisco)

Last updated: 2026-03-04

San Francisco (SFO) is known for efficient agriculture processing. At SFO: Cargo pet collection is usually at the cargo terminals near the north end of the airport. In-cabin pets clear customs in the International Terminal. SFO staff are highly experienced with CDC high-risk protocols, making it a preferred entry point for dogs from the Pacific region. Look for the outdoor pet relief area just outside the International Terminal arrivals.

Essential Preparation

  • ISO 11784/11785 compliant microchip
  • Rabies vaccination (at least 30 days before travel)
  • International health certificate (endorsed by local authorities)
  • IATA approved travel crate
