Bring a Dog from India to the USA (2026)

Last updated: 2026-03-04

High-Risk Alert: India is on the CDC's high-risk list. You MUST apply for a CDC Dog Import Permit well in advance. Dogs must be at least 6 months old and microchipped. Arrival is restricted to airports with CDC quarantine stations (like JFK, LAX, or ATL).

Essential Preparation

  • ISO 11784/11785 compliant microchip
  • Rabies vaccination (at least 30 days before travel)
  • International health certificate (endorsed by local authorities)
  • IATA approved travel crate
