Cat Import Requirements for the USA (2026)

Last updated: 2026-03-04

Compared to dogs, cat import rules for the United States are simple. The Essentials: There is no federal requirement for a rabies vaccine for cats, but almost all airlines and many US states require it. We strongly recommend an ISO microchip and a health certificate from your origin country's vet. Cats do not need a CDC permit, regardless of which country they are coming from.

Essential Preparation

  • ISO 11784/11785 compliant microchip
  • Rabies vaccination (at least 30 days before travel)
  • International health certificate (endorsed by local authorities)
  • IATA approved travel crate
