Do US States Have Different Pet Entry Rules?

While the CDC handles federal entry, individual states have mandates. Hawaii: Extremely strict 'Rabies-Free' rules; requires 4 months of prep. Guam: Requires pre-arrival quarantine. Mainland USA: Most states follow federal rules, but some require a local health certificate.
