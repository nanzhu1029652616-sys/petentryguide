Pet Microchip Requirements (ISO 11784/11785)

Last updated: 2026-03-04

Your pet must have a 15-digit ISO 11784/11785 compliant microchip. The chip must be implanted BEFORE the most recent rabies vaccine. If your pet has a non-ISO chip, you must carry your own scanner. The number must be clearly listed on all health records.

Essential Preparation

  • ISO 11784/11785 compliant microchip
  • Rabies vaccination (at least 30 days before travel)
  • International health certificate (endorsed by local authorities)
  • IATA approved travel crate
