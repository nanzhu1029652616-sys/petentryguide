How to Book a Flight for Your Pet (Step-by-Step)

Last updated: 2026-03-04

Booking a pet flight requires more than just buying a ticket. The 2026 Workflow: 1. Research pet-friendly airlines. 2. Call the airline to confirm pet space before booking your own seat. 3. Provide exact crate dimensions and total weight. 4. Request a 'Pet In Cabin' or 'AVIH' (animal in hold) SSR code on your booking. 5. Confirm the booking again 48 hours before departure.

Essential Preparation

  • ISO 11784/11785 compliant microchip
  • Rabies vaccination (at least 30 days before travel)
  • International health certificate (endorsed by local authorities)
  • IATA approved travel crate
