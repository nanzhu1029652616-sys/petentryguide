Bring a Cat from India to the USA (2026)

Expert Relocation Guide • Updated Mar 04, 2026

Cats from India need an ISO microchip and a rabies vaccination certificate. You must also obtain a No Objection Certificate (NOC) and an export health certificate from the Animal Quarantine and Certification Service (AQCS) in India. Cats do not require a CDC permit for the USA.
